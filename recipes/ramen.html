Ramen

Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodles
  • Pre-bought Broth From Someone More Patient
  • Naruto
  • Soft Boiled Egg
  • Cool Chopsticks

Steps to Follow

  1. Take the premade broth and bring it to a boil

  2. Add noodles and just prepare yourself for how loud you are about to slurp

  3. Add in the eggs and naruto, as well as any other topping you'd like

  4. It's done! And I wasn't kidding early. Slurp like your life depends on it.
